Fifir

Fifir is a popular Ethiopian breakfast dish that is made with injera (a spongy sourdough flatbread), spicy tomato sauce, and scrambled eggs. The dish is traditionally served with a side of fresh cheese, and can be customized with additional ingredients such as minced meat, onions, and green peppers. The combination of tangy injera, savory tomato sauce, and fluffy scrambled eggs makes Fifir a delicious and filling breakfast option.
